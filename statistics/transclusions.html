Wikisource Transclusion Statistics

This page shows how many texts at Wikisource are backed with scans, using the ProofreadPage extension. It is updated daily by a cron job.
Notes:
  • Some subdomains store author pages in the main namespace, which biases the percentage.
  • Most texts at the German language Wikisource are backed with scans, but do not use transclusion ; they are misrepresented here.
